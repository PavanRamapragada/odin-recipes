Pulihora

Description

This is a quite popular and simple telugu dish that is liked by many people. Below is the list of ingredients required to prepare the dish:

Ingredients

  • Aloo - 4
  • Oil - as per the standards
  • Salt - for taste
  • Chilly powder
  • misc
    • mustard seeds
    • green chillies
    • garam masala

Steps:

  1. Boil the potatoes and then peel the skin once they are cool
  2. Cut the aloo into cubes of preferred sizes
  3. Pour some oil in the pan and get it to heat
  4. Once the oil is hot enough, put all the aloo cubes in the pan and let it sautte for few minutes
  5. Ready your spices by the time the aloo in the pan comes to roast:
    • Salt
    • Chilly powder
    • Pepper powder
    • etc
